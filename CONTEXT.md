# CatStar

CatStar is a memorial companionship product for people grieving a cat who has died. Its language centers on gentle remembrance, delayed letters, and a small continuing presence from the cat's imagined life on Cat Star.

## Language

**喵星来信**:
The canonical Chinese product name. It refers to the whole memorial letter experience, not just the mailbox or the cat scene.
_Avoid_: CatStar as user-facing Chinese name

**Letters from Cat Star**:
The English display name for **喵星来信**. Use it for English-facing product surfaces, not as the internal context name.
_Avoid_: CatStar as polished English display name

**喵星**:
The imagined place where the **小猫** now lives and sends letters from. It is a setting inside the product, not the product name itself.
_Avoid_: 产品名, 账号空间

**小猫**:
The real cat once cared for by the user and now deceased. The product treats the cat as a remembered companion, not as a generic virtual pet or game character.
_Avoid_: 宠物, 虚拟宠物, 电子宠物, 游戏角色, AI 宠物

**用户**:
The person who cared for the **小猫** and now receives letters from it. In user-facing copy, this person may be addressed as the cat's human, family member, or owner depending on tone.
_Avoid_: 玩家, 账号, 客户

**家人称呼**:
The name or nickname the **小猫** uses for the **用户** in letters. User-facing copy should ask how the cat should address the person, not frame the person as an owner.
_Avoid_: 主人名, owner, 饲主

**喜欢的零食**:
The treat or food the **小猫** especially liked. During Phase 0, this is intentionally narrower than favorite things or habits so letter wording stays concrete.
_Avoid_: 喜欢的东西, 爱好, 习惯

**纪念特征**:
Real remembered traits of the **小猫** recorded in the **护照**, such as coat color and personality. These traits may shape **陪伴表现**, but they are not game stats or customization skins.
_Avoid_: 皮肤, 属性, 配置, 角色参数

**信件**:
A prewritten memorial companionship text from the **小猫** to the **用户**, delivered into the **时光信箱** over time and lightly personalized with passport fields. A letter is not generated by AI during Phase 0 and is not a live chat reply.
_Avoid_: AI 生成内容, 聊天回复, 实时对话

**最终信**:
The last **信件** in the current letter script. It may only be opened after all other letters have been read; after reading it, the **用户** makes a gentle farewell choice to complete the active **告别** process.
_Avoid_: 彩蛋, 结局关卡, 终极奖励

**护照**:
The memorial identity of one **小猫** in the CatStar world. During Phase 0, the experience maintains one current passport and uses it to personalize letters and the companionship scene.
_Avoid_: 账号, 存档, 档案表, profile

**重新登记**:
The act of clearing the current **护照** and starting the registration flow again. It must be framed as replacing the current memorial setup, not deleting the **小猫**.
_Avoid_: 删除小猫, 重置游戏, 清档

**本地纪念数据**:
The memorial information kept for the current device during Phase 0, including the **护照** and letter reading progress. It is private to the device and should not be described as an account, cloud archive, or synced profile.
_Avoid_: 云存档, 账号资料, 同步档案

**具体回忆**:
A personal story or detailed memory about the **小猫** and the **用户**. Phase 0 does not collect concrete memories because **信件** are prewritten and should not imply knowledge the product does not use.
_Avoid_: 回忆素材, 私人故事库, 记忆训练

**轻柔祝愿**:
A supportive phrase that offers care without instructing the **用户** how to grieve. Letters may offer companionship and hope, but must not command the user to stop feeling sad or become happy.
_Avoid_: 你一定要快乐, 别难过了, 快点走出来

**告别**:
The gentle memorial process completed through delayed **信件**. It is not disappearance, deletion, or breaking contact; it is the movement from active letters into quiet remembrance.
_Avoid_: 通关, 毕业, 结束服务

**星河陪伴**:
The quiet memorial state after the final **信件** has been read. The **小猫** remains present in the scene and may continue ordinary companionship expressions, but no longer sends new letters.
_Avoid_: 定格陪伴, 养成完成, 永久聊天, 内容续订

**星尘标记**:
A soft star-like memorial mark that may appear near the **小猫** after the **最终信** is read. It belongs to the Cat Star setting and should not imply angels, ascension, or religious symbolism.
_Avoid_: 光环, 天使, 升天, 成仙

**陪伴表现**:
The visible actions and moods of the **小猫** in the scene. These expressions show that the **小猫** is still gently present; they are not a game mechanic, task state, or growth system.
_Avoid_: 宠物养成, 玩法, 任务状态, 技能状态

**陪伴反应**:
A short non-narrative response shown when the **用户** interacts with the **小猫**. It creates presence but does not advance the letter story, invent memories, or become a chat system.
_Avoid_: 聊天, 回复, 剧情文本, AI 对话

**时光信箱**:
The place where prewritten **信件** are delivered according to real elapsed days since the **护照** was created. It is an intentionally paced memorial ritual, not a task list or reward track.
_Avoid_: 任务列表, 收件箱, 关卡, 奖励

**投递**:
The moment when the next prewritten **信件** arrives in the **时光信箱** and becomes available to read. During Phase 0, the first letter is delivered immediately after the **护照** is created; later letters arrive at 8:00 AM in the user's device-local time starting on the next calendar day. Delivery accumulates even if the app is closed, and 投递 does not mean the **用户** has read the letter or imply AI generation.
_Avoid_: 生成, 解锁, 完成, 领取, 打卡

**已读**:
The state applied when the **用户** opens a delivered **信件**. Phase 0 does not require a separate confirmation button to mark a letter as read.
_Avoid_: 确认读完, 完成任务, 领取

**告别选择**:
The small ritual choice shown at the end of the **最终信**. It lets the **用户** thank the **小猫** for the companionship and enter **星河陪伴** without an interruptive pre-read warning.
_Avoid_: 打开前警告, 确认弹窗, 通关按钮

**信箱封存**:
The state of the **时光信箱** after every existing **信件** has been read. It stops producing new letters, but still allows the **用户** to revisit letters that have already been read.
_Avoid_: 禁用, 关闭, 删除

**离世日期**:
The memorial date when the **小猫** left the real world. During Phase 0, this date is remembered in the **护照** but does not determine letter delivery timing.
_Avoid_: 投递起点, 计时起点

**护照创建时间**:
The moment when the **用户** begins the CatStar **告别** ritual for one **小猫**. During Phase 0, this time determines **信件** delivery timing.
_Avoid_: 离世日期, 注册时间

## Example Dialogue

Dev: Can a user create a cat that is still alive?
Domain expert: No. A **小猫** in CatStar is a real cat who has died. The experience is about remembrance and gentle companionship after loss.

Dev: Should Phase 0 support dogs or other pets?
Domain expert: No. **喵星来信** is specifically about cats. Do not generalize **小猫** into a generic pet model during Phase 0.

Dev: Are letters generated from the user's memories?
Domain expert: No. A **信件** is prewritten and only lightly personalized. Phase 0 should not pretend to know memories the user did not enter.

Dev: Can the final letter be read before earlier letters?
Domain expert: No. The **最终信** must be read last, after every other **信件** has been read.

Dev: Should the app warn the user before opening the final letter?
Domain expert: No. Let the **用户** read it first, then show a **告别选择** at the end.

Dev: What if the user closes the final letter before the farewell choice?
Domain expert: The **最终信** can stay **已读**, but **星河陪伴** does not begin until the **告别选择** is completed.

Dev: Does the user need to confirm that a letter is finished?
Domain expert: No. In Phase 0, opening a delivered **信件** marks it as **已读**.

Dev: Is the passport the user's account?
Domain expert: No. A **护照** belongs to one **小猫**. It is a memorial identity, not an account system.

Dev: Can the app offer a reset button?
Domain expert: Only carefully. Call it **重新登记** and require clear confirmation; never frame it as deleting the **小猫**.

Dev: Does Phase 0 sync the passport across devices?
Domain expert: No. Phase 0 keeps **本地纪念数据** on the current device only.

Dev: Should the onboarding form ask for memories with the cat?
Domain expert: No. Phase 0 does not collect **具体回忆**, because letters are prewritten and should not pretend to use private memories.

Dev: Can a letter tell the user they must be happy?
Domain expert: No. Use **轻柔祝愿**. The product should not pressure the **用户** to grieve correctly.

Dev: Should the form ask for the owner's name?
Domain expert: No. Ask for the **家人称呼**, meaning how the **小猫** should address the **用户**.

Dev: Can the favorite snack field contain any favorite object?
Domain expert: No. In Phase 0, **喜欢的零食** means food or treats, keeping the letter wording specific.

Dev: Are coat color and personality just customization options?
Domain expert: No. They are **纪念特征**. They may affect how the **小猫** appears or moves, but they come from remembrance.

Dev: What happens after the final letter?
Domain expert: The experience enters **星河陪伴**. The **告别** is complete, but the **小猫** is still gently present through ordinary **陪伴表现**.

Dev: Can clicking the cat produce new story content?
Domain expert: No. It can show a **陪伴反应**, but the story belongs to **信件**.

Dev: Does the mailbox disappear after all letters are read?
Domain expert: No. **信箱封存** means no new letters are produced, but already-read **信件** remain available for rereading.

Dev: Can the final letter alone put the mailbox into sealed state?
Domain expert: No. **信箱封存** starts only after every existing **信件** has been read. The **用户** should not be pushed past unread letters.

Dev: Can users receive every letter immediately?
Domain expert: No. The **时光信箱** uses daily 8:00 AM **投递** in device-local time. Development shortcuts can exist, but the product experience should preserve the waiting ritual.

Dev: If the user does not open the app for several days, are those letters delayed?
Domain expert: No. **投递** accumulates by date. When the **用户** returns, all letters that should have arrived are in the **时光信箱**.

Dev: If the cat died years ago, should all letters arrive immediately?
Domain expert: No. **离世日期** is memorial information. **护照创建时间** starts the CatStar letter ritual.
